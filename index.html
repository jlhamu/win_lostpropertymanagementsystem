How WIN supports every step of lost property recovery

World-class lost property systems use consistent intake, clear ownership matching, and rapid returns. WIN applies this approach with campus-friendly workflows and secure reporting.

1

Capture found item details

Staff log item descriptions, location, date and sender details into a searchable digital record, eliminating paper forms.

2

Validate and safely store

Each item receives a secure reference, storage location, and condition report so assets are protected while awaiting claim.

3

Notify potential owners

Automated alerts and targeted searches help connect students and staff with their lost items faster than manual outreach.

4

Confirm identity and release

Secure claim verification, receipt issuance, and audit logging ensure returned property is correctly matched and documented.
